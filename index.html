How to Use TemplateMagic

Create clean UV/laser templates in a few quick steps.

  1. Upload your image using Upload Image.
  2. Use Auto Wand for one-click outlines, or Manual for point-by-point tracing.
  3. Adjust edge/noise settings to improve detection quality.
  4. Add a custom fill texture (optional) under Output Fill Settings.
  5. Export your finished template as SVG or PNG.
